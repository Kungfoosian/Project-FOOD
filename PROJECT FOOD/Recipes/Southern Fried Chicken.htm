Southern Fried Chicken

Ingredients:

Name Amount Have?
Bone-in chicken w/ skin n/a
Buttermilk n/a
All-purpose flour n/a
Eggs 6
Milk n/a
Tony’s Cajun Seasoning heavy
Onion salt heavy
Lard 2 blocks (2lbs?)
Salt Post-cook seasoning

Directions:

1. Marinade chicken overnight in buttermilk.
2. Take chicken out and coat with All-purpose flour.
3. Mix leftover buttermilk with six eggs and milk.
4. Mix large container of all purpose flour with heavy doses onion salt and Tony’s Cajun seasoning.
5. Dip flour coated chicken in egg/milk mixture and immediately coat in another layer of the seasoned flour (from step 4, hard press the flour into the chicken like you're choking it).
6. Melt two blocks of lard in a large iron skillet. When lard starts to smoke, start cooking chicken. If there is no sizzle when cooking the chicken, lard isn’t hot enough.
7. When finished, place on a drain rack and salt both sides.
